Examples

In the following we list the demo files provided by the nanobem toolbox, which are used elsewhere in the help pages.

Contents

  • Base classes
  • galerkin
  • galerkinstat

Base classes

  • demomaterial01.m - Materials within nanobem toolobox.
  • demoparticle01.m - Plot elementary particle shapes.
  • demopoint01.m - Place points in environment of sphere.
  • demopoint02.m - Place points in environment of coupled spheres.
  • demoquad01.m - Quadrature rules for triangle.
  • demoquadduffy01.m - Quadrature rules for identical and touching triangles.
  • demoquadduffy02.m - Apply quadrature rules for Duffy integration.

galerkin

  • demoboundaryedge01.m - Plot Raviart-Thomas shape elements.
  • democalderon01.m - Calderon matrix for nanosphere.
  • demoretdip01.m - Lifetime of dipole above nanosphere.
  • demoretfield01.m - Electric field of optically excited nanosphere.
  • demoretfield02.m - Electric field of optically excited coupled nanospheres.
  • demoretspec01.m - Optical spectrum for metallic nanosphere.
  • demoretspec02.m - Optical spectra for coupled nanospheres.

Simulations with resonance modes

  • democim01.m - Resonance modes for ellipsoid and planewave excitation.
  • democim02.m - Resonance modes for ellipsoid and dipole excitation.
  • democim03.m - Resonance modes for dielectric nanosphere and nonresonant background.
  • demochar01.m - Characteristic modes for optically excited ellipsoid.

Simulations with Feibelman parameters

  • demofeibel01.m - Optical spectra nanospheres.
  • demofeibel02.m - Electric field of optically excited nanospheres.
  • demofeibel03.m - Resonance modes for ellipsoid and planewave excitation.

Simulations with stratified media

  • demostratlayer01.m - Primary and secondary fields in layerstructure.
  • demostratlayer02.m - Electromagnetic fields for planewave excitation in layerstructure.
  • demostratsomint01.m - Sommerfeld integration, plot integrand.
  • demostratsomint02.m - Sommerfeld integration, plot integrand w/o quasistatic contribution.
  • demostratgreen01.m - Tabulated Green function for sphere above substrate.
  • demostratspec01.m - Spectrum for gold sphere above glass substrate.
  • demostratspec02.m - Spectrum for gold disk on top of glass substrate.
  • demostratfield01.m - Electric field for optically excited nanosphere.
  • demostratfield02.m - Electric field for optically excited nanodisk.
  • demostratfar01.m - Electric farfields for optically excited nanosphere.
  • demostratfar02.m - Scattering spectra for detector with finite NA.
  • demostratdip01.m - Decay rate for dipole and gold sphere above substrate.
  • demostratdip02.m - LDOS enhancement for gold sphere above substrate.

Simulations with multipole expansion

  • demomulti01.m - T-matrix for dielectric nanosphere and single wavelength.
  • demomulti02.m - T-matrices for TiO2 nanodisk and multiple wavelengths.

galerkinstat

  • demoboundaryvert01.m - Plot linear shape elements.
  • demostatdip01.m - Lifetime of dipole above nanosphere.
  • demostatfield01.m - Electric field of optically excited nanosphere.
  • demostatfield02.m - Electric field of optically excited coupled nanospheres.
  • demostatspec01.m - Optical spectrum for metallic nanosphere.
  • demostatspec02.m - Optical spectra for coupled nanospheres.

Simulations with quasistatic eigenmodes

  • demostateig01.m - Quasistatic eigenmodes for nanosphere.
  • demostateig02.m - Quasistatic eigenmodes for nanoellipsoid.
  • demostateig03.m - Quasistatic eigenmodes for nanocube.
