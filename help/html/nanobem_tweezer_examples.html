Examples for +tweezer classes

In the following we list the demo files for the +tweezer features of the nanobem toolbox.

Base classes

  • demoforce01.m - Force on spherical particle, comparison T-matrix and BEM.
  • demoforce02.m - Force on spherical particle, 2d map.
  • demoforce03.m - Force and torque for ellipsoid in OF2i setup.
  • demostokes01.m - Stokes drag for ellipsoid.

Optical tweezer simulations

  • demotweezer01.m - Tweezer simulation for spherical particle.
  • demotweezer02.m - Tweezer simulation for ellipsoidal particle.

Optofluidic force induction (OF2i) simulations

  • demomiefield01.m - Fields around sphere in OF2i setup.
  • demotrapsph01.m - Trapping position for spherical walkers in OF2i setup.
  • demowalkersph01.m - Propagate spherical walkers in OF2i setup.
  • demowalkersph02.m - 3d plot for spherical walkers in OF2i setup.
  • demowalkersph03.m - Light scattering for spherical walkers in OF2i setup.
  • demowalkersph04.m - Histogram for Brownian motion and spheres.
  • demowalkerpol01.m - Propagate ellipsoidal walkers in OF2i setup.
  • demowalkerpol02.m - 3d plot of ellipsoidal walkers in OF2i setup.
  • demowalkerpol03.m - Light scattering for ellipsoidal walkers in OF2i setup.
  • demowalkerpol04.m - Same as demowalkerpol03.m but with Brownian loop.
  • demowalkerpol05.m - Histogram for Brownian motion and ellipsoids.
